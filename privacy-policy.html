  • Where and How We Store Data: Your data is securely stored on encrypted servers in India or approved cloud platforms, with strict access controls.
  • Data Retention: We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as mandated by applicable laws (e.g., tax records, legal compliance).

    • Regular security audits and malware scans.

Under Indian data protection frameworks and global privacy best practices, you are entitled to:

For exercising these rights, please contact us at privacy@aquapulse.co.in.
